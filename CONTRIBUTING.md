# Contributing to GEO Optimization Handbook

Contributions are welcome! This handbook aims to stay current with the rapidly evolving GEO landscape.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Create a branch** for your change: `git checkout -b my-change`
3. **Make your changes** — add new content, fix errors, update platform docs
4. **Submit a Pull Request**

## What Kind of Contributions Are Helpful

- Updates to AI search platform behavior (platforms change frequently)
- New citation strategies or research findings
- Corrections to factual errors
- Translations to other languages
- Real-world case studies and results
- Additional practical guides for specific platforms

## Content Guidelines

- Keep sections scannable with clear headings and bullet points
- Cite sources for statistics and claims
- Use concrete examples over abstract theory
- Separate methodology (what works) from opinion (what might work)
- Mark speculative content clearly

## Style

- Use plain English
- Keep sentences short and direct
- Prefer active voice
- One idea per paragraph
